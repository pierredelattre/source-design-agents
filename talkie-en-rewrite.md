## Section 2 - User research

**Images:** `per.jpg`, `entr.jpg`, `fonctions.jpg`

> ✅ The visuals already carry profiles and insights. The text should keep concrete friction points and the shared conclusion.

**Before:**

> Three 30-minute semi-structured interviews with active users of competing apps.
>
> The first uses Duolingo to prepare for travel. He feels blocked: he cannot skip mastered topics and often confuses similar words.
>
> The second uses MemRise for work. He stopped because exercises were too simple, repetitive, and gave no clear view of progress.
>
> The third wants job-specific vocabulary. He only finds generic content, not adapted to his needs.
>
> Shared point: all started motivated. The issue was not engagement, but lack of perceived progress.

**After:**

> Three 30-minute interviews with active users of competing apps.
>
> The same issues kept coming back: hard to skip what is already mastered, repetitive exercises, overly generic content, and poor visibility on progress.
>
> The takeaway is simple: motivation was there, but progress did not feel visible enough.

**Cut:**
- Full per-profile detail already readable in research assets
- Repetitive descriptive passages in each individual case
